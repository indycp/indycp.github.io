About Our Team

The Independence CyberPatriot Team was formed in 2023 by dedicated students at Independence High School in Ashburn, VA. We promote cybersecurity awareness, develop practical skills, and compete at the highest levels in national competitions.

Our Mission

To educate and empower the next generation of cybersecurity professionals through hands-on experience, collaboration, and competition excellence.
